About

This is a coloring book that is both digital and on paper.


By coloring on paper, readers can explore symmetry and the beauty of math. Follow along on the digital copy to bring the concepts and illustrations to life in interactive animations.



Printed copy: Coming soon

Digital copy: coloring-book.co



The illustrations in this book are designed by algorithms that follow the symmetry rules of the groups that the illustrations belong to. These algorithms also use generative art techniques to add components of randomness - notice the illustrations never repeat.
