There are more ways than just a 1/3 turn to rotate that triangle without changing it.

It can be rotated by twice that much - 2/3 of the way around the circle - or by 3 times that much, which is all the way around the circle.

We can keep rotating - by 4 times that much, 5 times that much, 6 times... and keep going. The triangle seems to have an infinite number of rotations, but after rotating 3 times they become repetitive.
